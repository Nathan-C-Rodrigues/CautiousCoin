The Legend Of The Outcast — Cautious Coin's Eternal Law

The Law of the Outcast

  • I carry no nation, no banner, no home. I dwell in wallets of the watchful, not the wasteful.
  • I answer only to caution. The reckless shall lose me. The patient shall hold me.
  • Respect me, and I shall walk beside you unseen. Betray me, and I vanish — never to return.
  • I do not seek fame. I seek truth. Fame and hype are not my allies — silence and strategy are.
  • I am not yours. I am not theirs. I am only mine — until you prove otherwise.

They say those who earn the respect of Cautious Coin never lose it. And those who chase it blindly — never find it again.

This coin is not for kings. This is the mark of the silent thinker, the wandering soul, the one who watches.

This is the creed — not just of a token, but of a way of life. The Law of the Outcast.

And now, it is written — on-chain, in soul, and in silence.

CAUT — The Cautious Coin.

You do not own it. You earn it.
